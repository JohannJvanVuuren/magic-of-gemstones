The magic of gemstones

A short journey into the history, symbolic meaning and

healing properties of gemstones.

Introduction

Since the beginning of time, mankind have been held in the spell of the gems of the earth. In ancient time these stones were considered sacred and were integral in the domains of religion, medicine, protection and divinatory practices.

From prehistoric times we have evidence of the Shiva Linghams, which were used as symbolic presentations of the god Shiva.

The ancient Greek Pythagoreans believed that the sun was a giant crystal ball that gathered the light of the cosmos and reflected it back to the earth.

Gemstones have since time immemorial been thought of as the very bones of the earth and believed to carry the power of our life giving planet.

Our current theories revolves around the belief that they hold and transmute energy. It is this transmuted energy that interacts with the body, mind and soul of a living being.

In this website I seek to offer a brief introduction not only to stones and their mystical properties, but also the ancient Chakra system to which the various stones have been mapped.

It is this mapping of stones, their qualities and the corresponding Chakras, that form the basis of crystal healing as we know it today.

There are numerous anecdotal accounts of the benefit of crystal therapy and healing, which have ensured a thriving industry around it.

It is important to note, however, that the theories around gemstones and their mystical properties are intuitive and based on anecdotal evidence, and has not been subjected to empirical scientific investigation.

Having said that, it still remains a part of our heritage as human beings and has been a source of inspiration and relief for many people.

Sensual Magic

Crystals are a sensual and intuitive experience rather than an intellectual pursuit. They have a unique ability to absorb and transmute negative energy into beneficence - and to interact with us energetically. Their ability to harness the power of our mind is truly incredible - they amp up the energy , boosting whatever we do. And it's through handling them that we really connect to their magic.

If you've never worked with crystals before, you're in for a treat. These scrumptious beings pack a potent punch and you'll be amazed at the ways in which they enhance your life. Not merely decorative, they are protective and life-affirming too. They can increase your energy, enhance your well-being, help you to keep calm in a crisis, change the way you think, ensure your space is safe, offer you guidance and much else besides.

To begin with, just pick up a Quartz crystal. Hold it lightly in your hand for a minute or two. Do you feel a tingling vibration or a pulsing in you hand? If so, you're experiencing the energy of the crystal.
