Selected stones and their properties

Amethyst

Tranquility, clarity of thought, enhances intuition and eases obsessive compulsive behaviour.

Aquamarine

Courage, release of anxiety and fear; Promotes insight and mental clarity

Black Obsidian

Associated with mystery and a strong ability to absorb; used for psychich cleansing and protection

Black Tourmaline

Symbolizes safety, grounding and protection; cover the wearer with a cloak to shield from negative energy.

Blue Lace Agate

Symbolises unity, hope, protection, harmony, optimism, positivity and appreciation of the natural world.

Citrine

Excellent for overcoming depression, fears and phobias. Also a powerfull stone for self-healing and insppiration.

Fluorite

Transforms negative energy into positive energy. Boosts mental clarity and promotes peace of mind. Good for spiritual detoxification.

Green Malachite

A protection stone. Absorbing negative energies and pollutants from the atmosphere and body. Also shields against electromagnetic fields.

Hematite

Protection, grounding, power and strength. Restores, strenthens and regulates blood flow.

Labradorite

One of the most powerfull protectors. Creates a shield for auras and protects against negative energies. Also tempers the negativities in oneself.

Lapis Lazuli

Brings deep peace, harmoney and reveals inner truth, honesty, compassion, self-awareness and self-expression. It is known as the wisdom stone.

Red Garnet

Represents love and commitment. Enhances sexuality, bringing warmth, devotion, understanding, trust and honesty to a relationship.

Red Jasper

Known as the supreme nurturer it supports and sustains during times of stress and brings tranquility and wholeness

Rose quartz

Known as the crystal of unconditional love. It boosts feelings of self-love and foster loving relationships with others.

Shungite

A powerful purification stone. Called the miracle stone because its a catalyst for growth, transformation and positive change.

Smoky Quartz

Assists to release old patterns of thought and behaviour. It is known to draw energy from the crown chakra to the root chakra helping to ground you.

Sodalite

Brings order and calmness to the mind. It encourages rational thought, objectivity, truth and intuition. It is also known to calm panic attacks.

Sunstone

Stone of leadership, encouraging the wearer to be open, benefolent and willing to bestow blessings onto others. It is also knows as the stone of joy.

Tigers Eye

Represents the power, courage and fearlessness of the tiger. It is the stone of courage, power and protection.

White howlite

Strengthens memory and stimulates desire for knowledge. It teaches patience and eliminate rage, pain and stress.
